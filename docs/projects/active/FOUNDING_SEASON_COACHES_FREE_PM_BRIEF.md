# PM Brief — Everything Free Until 2027 (Tournament Plus + Premium Coaches Portal)

**What we're doing:** Making the whole paid experience free for the rest of 2026 — Tournament Plus already is (Founding Season), and now the Premium Coaches Portal joins it at $0 until January 1, 2027 — while fixing every broken piece of the money path so nothing can charge anyone improperly this year.

**Why it matters:** The 2026 goal is users on the platform giving feedback, not revenue. Free pricing honestly frames the product's beta feel: people tolerate rough edges and iterate with us when they aren't paying. Every signup this year is a feedback channel and a January conversion candidate.

**What users see and do differently:**
- **Coaches**: "Coach a team" stops saying "coming soon." A coach taps it, creates a free team home (roster, schedule, fees, announcements, chat, tournament history) in one short form, and can upgrade to the full Premium portal at $0 — with an honest "Free until Jan 1, 2027 — then $29/mo" label everywhere the price appears.
- **Organizers**: unchanged happy path (free tournament → $0 Tournament Plus), but the "add payment method" step now truly only saves a card — it no longer quietly starts a subscription that would have billed people in November. And the broken little things on the way in (invisible tap traps, dead-end marketing pages, missing sign-in links) are fixed.
- **Nobody can be charged in 2026.** The one live checkout that could have charged $29 (accidentally open on production today) gets closed immediately, then reopened only as the $0 promo.

**Customer impact:** Lower-friction acquisition on both doors (organizer + coach), honest beta framing, and a single clean promise — "everything is free until January 1, 2027" — that matches what the product actually does.

**January 2027 (deliberately manual):** No automation. The owner mass-emails the cohort in the fall ("put a card on file by Dec 31"), and in January personally converts, downgrades, or cancels each account. At current volume (one org) this is comfortable; we revisit automation only if the cohort outgrows hand-conversion.

**Priority:** Highest — it unblocks the acquisition funnel work already reviewed and removes live billing risk.

**Success criteria:**
1. A coach can self-serve from the app's "Coach a team" door to a working free team, and to a $0 Premium workspace, with no card.
2. No surface can charge a real card before Jan 1, 2027; the only card interaction is an explicit save-for-later.
3. Pricing copy, plan config, and the canonical Facts doc all tell the same story (drift check passes).
4. A written January runbook exists that one person can execute.
