# Club Repackaging — PM Brief

**What it does:** Re-prices the Club plan from a single $179 rate plus a $19/month charge per coaching team beyond three, into **two simple size bands** — **Club** for clubs up to 15 teams (~$219/mo) and **Club Large / Association** for 15–30 teams (~$379/mo), with custom pricing above 30. Every coach on a club's staff gets the full Premium Coaches Portal at no extra per-team cost. The standalone $29 Coaches Portal for unaffiliated coaches is unchanged.

**Why it matters:** Research shows the typical Club buyer runs 8–12 teams (median ~10), so the old "3 included + $19 each" structure quietly turned an advertised $179 into a real ~$270–350 — and it charged orgs for the one thing that makes them stick: getting their whole coaching staff into the platform. Bands are predictable (volunteer treasurers hate variable bills), they fit a 5-team club and a 30-team association without forcing a segment choice, and they stop penalizing the coach adoption we actually want.

**Who benefits:** Club-tier organizations and their coaching staffs. No change for Tournament, Tournament Plus, League, or standalone Coaches Portal buyers.

**Expected impact:** A club sees one honest number for its size, with the whole staff included. Big associations stop being under-served by a too-low flat price; small clubs stop being priced out. Internally, the per-team billing machinery is retired in favour of size bands.

**Priority:** High — it's the structural foundation the Club early-access cohort will be sold on, and it removes a live contradiction between the plan card and the comparison table. Sequenced ahead of opening Club for sale.

**Success criteria:**
- A Club org's whole coaching staff has Premium portals with no per-team charge.
- Team-count capacity is enforced at the 15 / 30 band boundaries; over-cap adds prompt an upgrade (or a conversation above 30).
- Pricing surfaces show two bands and no "$19 per extra team" line anywhere.
- No existing customer loses team access in the cutover.
