# PM Brief — Public Tournament "Wow" Upgrade

## What this is
The public tournament page is the single biggest reason organizers buy FieldLogicHQ — it's what they hand to their coaches, parents, and fans. This project makes that experience feel premium and *alive* so the audience walks away thinking *"this was the best tournament experience I've ever had."*

## Proposed functionality (Phase 1 — free for every tier)
- **The page comes alive on game day.** When a tournament is happening today, the schedule, standings, and home pages refresh themselves automatically — scores update and visibly "flip" without anyone hitting reload. Off-event-day nothing polls, so there's no wasted load.
- **A "My Tournament" card.** After a parent taps to follow their team (no account, same as today), they get a personal card: next game with a live countdown, current record, standings position, live status — plus a one-tap **"Add my team's games to my calendar."**
- **A more premium look.** A coordinated visual polish pass (richer cards, depth, smoother motion, a bolder LIVE treatment) on the schedule and home pages.

## Coming in Phase 2 — the upgrade carrot
- **Fan score alerts (Tournament Plus).** Followers get a push notification the moment their team's score is posted — even though they never made an account — plus an "Add to Home Screen" install prompt. This is the one signature feature reserved for the paid tier; everything else stays free.

## Why it matters
- The public page is the best-selling feature; making it unforgettable drives word-of-mouth and renewals.
- "Scores update live" and "alerts when my kid's team finishes" are the two things parents consistently want and competitors rarely deliver at this price.
- Hybrid tiering keeps the free experience genuinely great (good for acquisition) while giving Tournament Plus a concrete, emotional reason to upgrade.

## Customer impact
- **Parents/fans:** less refreshing, less hunting; their team's day at a glance; calendar sync; (Plus) instant score alerts.
- **Coaches:** a polished, shareable page that makes their event look professional.
- **Organizers:** a stronger sales/renewal story and a clear Plus upsell.

## Priority & sequencing
1. **Phase 1 (now):** live refresh + My Tournament card + calendar + visual polish — all free.
2. **Phase 1.5:** live refresh on the single-game page.
3. **Phase 2:** fan push alerts + PWA install (Plus-gated) + finish the visual pass.
4. **Deferred:** photo galleries, per-player stats.

## Success criteria
- On game day, a followed team's score change is visible on the public pages within ~30s with no manual refresh and no visual flicker.
- A fan can add their team's games to their phone calendar in one tap.
- (Phase 2) A fan on a Plus tournament receives a push notification within seconds of a score being posted, with no account required.
- No regressions to existing follow, division filters, mobile layouts, or per-org branding/light mode.
