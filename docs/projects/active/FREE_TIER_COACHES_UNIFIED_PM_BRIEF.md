# Free Tier + Coaches Experience — Unified PM Brief

**Status:** Scoped 2026-06-08. Execution plan: [FREE_TIER_COACHES_UNIFIED_PLAN.md](FREE_TIER_COACHES_UNIFIED_PLAN.md)

## What we're doing

Two efforts — making **every operator type start free** (tournament organizer, coach, house-league admin) and making the **coach portal a real product** — share the same coach code. We merged them into one ordered build plan so they don't collide and can be executed phase by phase.

## What changes for customers

- A **coach** can create a free team home with no tournament — roster, schedule, messaging, fee tracking — and it carries over if their club later joins. (This is the #1 organic path to Club.)
- A **house-league admin** can run one small season free (a capped beta), with a shareable public schedule/standings page.
- A **tournament organizer** keeps the free event flow, with honest copy and an account that stops calling itself an "organization."
- A **tournament coach** gets the full game-day experience (status, roster submission, live Team HQ, afterglow).
- **Club** stays paid and consultative.
- **No money moves online yet** — manual fee tracking only; payments are a later, separate enhancement.

## Order of delivery (one list)

0 trust/copy cleanup → 1 coach foundation (org-less team route + portal fixes) → 2 account-first start + add-workspace → 3 coach master roster → 4 standalone coach floor → 5 tournament coach experience → 6 League Starter beta (caps) → 7 instrumentation → 8 abuse controls → 9 marketing flip. **Premium go-live, payments, and the Club trial are a deferred Future Rail.**

## Why it matters

It turns three dead-end personas into real free users, neutralizes the free-core competitors, and turns coaches into advocates that pull clubs in — without overpromising payments or undercutting paid League/Club.

## Success criteria

Every operator type has a working free self-serve path; coach + league-admin signups move off zero; conversions happen at fair "you've grown" walls; coach→club advocacy is tracked; no marketing surface advertises something that isn't live.

## Role-based access

No new admin roles. The changes are **which free entry each persona lands on** and the ability to **add another free workspace under one login**. Existing org roles are unchanged.
