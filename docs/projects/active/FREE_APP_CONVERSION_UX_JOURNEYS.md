# Step 2 — Owner Journey Scripts (run on your real phone)

Six short journeys, one per persona, on your actual phone (push/install/thumb-feel are the point — the agents can't judge those). Each has a goal, a path, and 2–3 taste questions. Sign out between journeys. Accounts + password in FREE_APP_CONVERSION_UX_FINDINGS.md (fixtures re-seeded pristine; re-run the live-tournament seeder first if no games are live today).

---

## Journey 1 — The grandparent (no account, ~5 min)
**You are:** Ava's grandmother. You heard "she's playing in Milton this weekend." You have the app installed but no account.
**Path:** open the app → Discover → search for the live demo event → find a live score → follow a team ("just this device" when nudged) → check Following tab → go back into the tournament → now try to get back to Discover *without* the back button.
**Taste questions:**
1. At the moment the account nudge appeared — annoyed, neutral, or fair enough?
2. When you tried to get "home" from inside the tournament — how long before you gave up or used back? (This is decision N1.)
3. Did anything make you feel you *needed* an account when you didn't?

## Journey 2 — The parent who wants alerts (~7 min)
**You are:** a parent who wants a buzz when the game goes live. Start signed out, fresh browser/app state.
**Path:** tournament page → find the alerts pitch (bell, or the alerts control near the schedule) → follow it → sign in as **Fiona** → confirm you land back where you started → turn alerts on for this device → open Account → Notification settings and look at the Followed teams card.
**Taste questions:**
1. Was "sign in to get score alerts" a fair ask at that moment — would real-you have done it at the diamond?
2. After sign-in, did the page *feel* like yours? (Look at her followed teams on the Teams tab — do the buttons match reality? This is decision N2.)
3. Push test: with alerts on, have a score change (or use the device tester) — did the buzz arrive?

## Journey 3 — Fiona's daily check (~4 min)
**Path:** sign in as Fiona from scratch → note where you land → check both teams' states → tap through to a game → back → Account tab → Your FieldLogicHQ → sign out.
**Taste questions:**
1. Sign-in → seeing her teams: fast enough to be a habit?
2. Anything on Account/switcher that feels like operator noise for a pure fan?

## Journey 4 — Riley claims their team (~6 min)
**Path:** sign in as **Riley** → notice where you land and what's offered → claim Bears U11 → look at what you see FIRST after claiming (this is decision C5 — the "Schedule: None" moment) → find today's game from there.
**Taste questions:**
1. Did the claim offer feel trustworthy or phishy?
2. After claiming — did you feel "this is useful" within 10 seconds, or lost?

## Journey 5 — Casey's game day + the $29 question (~8 min)
**Path:** sign in as **Casey** → from the team page find the public live score (Fan view) → flip back via the chip (one-handed!) → then: Explore tab → read the Premium pitch → tap Upgrade → stop at the pay screen (don't pay) — read it on your phone.
**Taste questions:**
1. The fan⇄coach flip: would you do this between innings?
2. On the coach pages you saw "Follow this team" — what did you expect it to do? (Decision N3.)
3. At the pay screen: did you know exactly what you'd get for $29/mo before you tapped? Where SHOULD this pitch have found you? (Decisions C3/C4.)

## Journey 6 — The rival organizer (~5 min)
**You are:** a parent at someone else's free-tier event thinking "I could run this better." Signed out.
**Path:** open `free-test-org/free-cup` on your phone → read the bottom banner → tap "Run an event" (should work now — it was untappable before this sweep) → follow the path to the signup form → stop before creating anything.
**Taste questions:**
1. Did the banner read as aimed at YOU, or at coaches? (Decision C6.)
2. If the banner weren't there — could you have found "run a tournament" on your phone at all?
