# PM Brief — Insights Weekly Digest + Smarter Findings

> Status: Approved from browser mockup 2026-07-10 — in build.

**What it does:** Every Sunday evening, a coach gets one notification with the week's headlines from their team's Insights — "Won 3 straight · Riley's over his pitching cap · 3 families haven't paid" — tappable straight into the full picture. And the "What stands out" list on Insights learns four new tricks: it warns before a dues installment comes due, spots when practices are being skipped even though games are packed, flags a position only one player has covered, and celebrates momentum and milestone wins.

**Why it matters:** Insights only works when a coach thinks to open it. The digest flips that — the insights come to the coach at the moment of the week they're most reflective, building a weekly habit around the page. The new rules deepen the page's core promise (the system reads the reports so the coach doesn't have to) using data the product already holds.

**Who benefits:** Premium Coaches Portal head coaches and assistants. Each person's digest is built only from what they're allowed to see — an assistant without money access simply never gets a money line. The digest is switchable off per-person like any other notification type. No plan or pricing change.

**Expected impact:** Weekly engaged-return visits to Insights without any prompting from us; earlier warnings on money and arm-care issues; the "smarter every season" story becomes visible — the page's shape never changes while its judgment improves.

**Priority:** Medium-High — cheap rides on finished rails (the findings engine and notification system both exist), with an outsized engagement payoff.

**Success criteria:** A coach receives the Sunday push, taps it, and lands on Insights with every claim verifiable; a quiet week sends nothing; an assistant's digest never contains a line their permissions wouldn't show them on the page; all engine changes fully unit-tested; owner verifies a manually-triggered digest on their own device.
