# Facilitated Payments (Scoping) — PM Brief

**What it does:** Kicks off the formal scoping (Q4 2026) of letting families/participants pay their org *through* FieldLogicHQ, with the platform earning a small take on each transaction — the model every dominant Canadian competitor uses. This brief covers the **scoping effort**, not a built feature; the build plan comes out the other end for an H1 2027 launch.

**Why it matters:** A flat subscription alone leaves the biggest source of value — money flowing through the platform — uncaptured, and it's a hard sell against free competitors. Payment processing is also the cleanest way to scale revenue with club size and to fund the free floors. But it carries real regulatory and trust weight, so it must be scoped carefully and must **not** launch into the free founding season.

**Who benefits:** Orgs (simpler collection, fewer chase-the-cheque cycles), families (pay online), and the business (a usage-based revenue stream). Eventually all paid segments; first money flow TBD in scope.

**Expected impact (of the scoping phase):** A decided revenue model — the actual take-rate (resolving the current margin-vs-all-in ambiguity), who pays, the processor, and the legal posture — plus a build-ready plan handed to `/plan`.

**Priority:** High strategically, but **deliberately sequenced** — scope in Q4 2026, launch H1 2027, after product hardening and clear of the Jan 2027 billing cliff.

**Success criteria:**
- A documented, non-ambiguous take-rate that is provably profitable per transaction and competitive all-in.
- A chosen processor and a counsel-reviewed compliance posture (KYC, refunds, chargebacks, tax, PIPEDA).
- A build plan + PM brief ready for an H1 2027 implementation.
