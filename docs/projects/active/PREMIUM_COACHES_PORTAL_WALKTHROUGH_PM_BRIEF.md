# Premium Coaches Portal — Walkthrough (PM Brief)

**What this is:** A hands-on evaluation of the paid Coaches Portal — the experience a coach
lands in *after* upgrading. The upgrade journey itself (free → pay → land in Premium) is
already built and verified; this pass judges whether the destination earns the price.

**Why it matters:** Premium is a recurring per-team charge. The first sessions inside the
paid portal set whether a coach feels the upgrade was worth it or asks for a refund. We
want the roster, schedule, dues/budget, documents, chat, and season tools to feel complete,
honest, and obviously more capable than the free home — with the data they built for free
carried over intact.

**What we're checking:** Every Premium surface, walked in order — does it work, is it clear,
does it look finished, and does it hold up on mobile? Special attention to: data migrated
from the free team home being correct, sensible empty states for a brand-new Premium team,
and head-coach vs assistant-coach permissions behaving as intended.

**Customer impact:** Higher confidence at the moment of highest churn risk (first weeks of
paying), fewer "what do I get for this?" support tickets, and a cleaner story for the
eventual prod launch.

**Priority:** High — gates the Premium prod release alongside the existing release checklist.

**Success criteria:** Every surface walked; all Blocker/High findings resolved or
consciously deferred by the owner; the portal reads as a coherent paid product, not a
bundle of free tools with extras bolted on.
