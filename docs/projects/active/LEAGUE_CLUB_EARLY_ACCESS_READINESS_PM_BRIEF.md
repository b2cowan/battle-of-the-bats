# League + Club Early-Access Readiness — PM Brief

**What it does:** Defines the concrete, testable bar a real admin must clear before we open League and Club to a small, founder-managed early-access group (5–10 Club, 10–15 League) under the Founding-Season comp. It converts the principle "ready when a real admin can run it end-to-end" into an actual go/no-go checklist, and names the hardening work that has to land first.

**Why it matters:** League and Club can't be bought today, so the new Club pricing — bands, $219, whole-staff portals — is untested against a single paying club. A managed cohort gets real orgs onto the product (validation, testimonials, and the founding customers we'd later convert) without an open-door launch we can't yet support. The discipline is the point: open too early and we accelerate the billing cliff and burn word-of-mouth.

**Who benefits:** The business (willingness-to-pay validation on our highest-value tiers) and the cohort orgs (white-glove onboarding with founding pricing). Indirectly, every later Club/League buyer benefits from a product hardened against real use.

**Expected impact:** A clear, owner-ratified checklist; a baseline of what passes today vs what needs work; and a gated "open" moment that flips League/Club to purchasable only when the experience actually delivers on first use.

**Priority:** High — this is the gate that turns all the ratified Club pricing work into revenue and learning. Target ~August 2026, but the date follows the checklist.

**Success criteria:**
- A real admin completes the League and Club end-to-end flows unaided on a clean org.
- Pricing surfaces, Stripe prices, and the Founding comp are all live and verified for the cohort.
- Owner go/no-go passes; the cohort is invited and onboarded without a blocker that should have been caught.

**Dependencies:** House-League In-Season Trust, Billing & Accounting Coherence, Admin IA & Multi-Module Nav, and Club Repackaging — this plan coordinates them toward the open date rather than duplicating them.
