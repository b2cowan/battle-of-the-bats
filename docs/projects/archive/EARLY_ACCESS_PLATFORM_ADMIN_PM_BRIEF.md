# Early Access Platform Admin PM Brief

## Summary

Create a Platform Admin **Early Access** workspace for managing League and Club interest captured from the public site. The goal is to help FieldLogicHQ understand demand, prioritize beta/pilot outreach, and notify interested organizations when features are ready.

## Why It Matters

League and Club are intentionally not self-serve yet. The early-access modal now captures real demand, but the team needs an operational place to review those leads and act on them. Without this, early-access submissions become passive database records instead of a launch pipeline.

## Customer Impact

Prospects who raise their hand for League or Club get more relevant follow-up. FieldLogicHQ can contact the right organizations when House League, Accounting, Rep Teams, or public-site workflows are ready, instead of sending generic updates.

## Internal User Impact

Platform admins can:

- See all early-access leads in one place
- Filter by plan and feature interest
- Identify pilot candidates
- Track outreach status
- Export or copy segmented email lists
- Keep internal notes on lead quality and fit

## Priority

Medium-high before League/Club launch. It is not required for Tournament/Tournament Plus go-live, but it becomes important as soon as early-access submissions start coming in.

## MVP Success Criteria

- Every early-access submission is visible in Platform Admin.
- Admins can filter by League, Club, and specific feature interests.
- Admins can mark leads as contacted, pilot candidates, converted, not a fit, or do not contact.
- Admins can add internal notes.
- Admins can export or copy emails for a filtered segment.
- The workflow supports manual outreach without pretending to be a full marketing platform.

## Future Marketing Platform Scope

If FieldLogicHQ wants this to become a marketing platform, the next layer would include campaign sending, saved audience segments, unsubscribe handling, delivery tracking, campaign analytics, automated journeys, and conversion attribution. That is intentionally outside the MVP so the first version can stay simple and launch-focused.
